Day1:Arrival – Havelock Island (Sightseeing) (Appox 54 kms by sea – 1 ½ hrs)
Day Highlights:

Assistance on arrival at Veer Savarkar Airport, Port Blair with and transfer to Havelock jetty by inter island ferry to Havelock Island (40 kms by sea from Port Blair), Assistance at Havelock Jetty. Check in your hotel on arrival. Later Enjoy your day at the Radhanagar Beach on the western coast, also known as Number 7 Beach, is one of the most popular beaches on Havelock and was named "Best Beach in Asia" by TIME in 2004... Overnight in Havelock. 

(Please be noted that Makruzz Timings (PB – HV) are as follows: 8 AM and after that 2 PM – Guest will have to arrive at least 1 ½ Hrs prior to the mentioned timing)

Day3:Neil Island (Approx. 44 kms by sea – 1 ½ hrs) – Sightseeing
Day Highlights:

Day 03:12.03.21:  Post breakfast check out from the hotel and take the transfer to Havelock jetty by inter island ferry to Neil Island an island with clear turquoise sea and white sand beaches.”. Assistance at Neil Island Jetty. Check in to your hotel on arrival. Later visit Bharatpur Beach, one of the most beautiful beaches in Andaman. You can take a ride in a glass bottomed boat to see under water corals and marine life and other water activities (Optional – extra cost on actual on direct payment). Late afternoon visit the Laxmanpur Beach 2 to view the natural coral bridge and also go to Laxmanpur Beach 1 to view the sunset. Evening free or at own. Overnight in Neil Island.

Day4:Port Blair (Approx. 40 kms by sea – 1 ½ hrs) –Sightseeing
Day Highlights:

Day 04:13.03.21: Post breakfast Later departure and transfer to jetty to take the inter-island ferry to Port Blair. Assistance at Port Blair Jetty. Check in upon arrival. Later visit Cellular Jail (Museum & Monument). Evening to the Cellular Jail, a place of pilgrimage for all freedom loving people. One can also view the history of the heroic freedom struggle which is brought alive in a moving Light & Sound Show at the Cellular Jail. Return to hotel. Overnight in Port Blair. Overnight in Port Blair.
