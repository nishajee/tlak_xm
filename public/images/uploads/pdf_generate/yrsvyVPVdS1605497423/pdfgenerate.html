Kerala Tour - Akash Tanmane

Day1:Arrival At Cochin Airport And Transfer To Munnar
Day Highlights:

On arrival at Cochin Airport, you will be received by our local executive (Driver). After a brief meet and greet, proceed to Munnar (125 Kms / 3.5 Hrs). .On the way visit the water falls of Valara and Cheeyappara. On arrival at Munnar, check-in to your hotel. You can relax at your hotel for the day. In the evening, if time permits, you can visit evening cultural show or local market for shopping.

Additional Info:

On arrival, driver will receive you at Airport. En-route you can have lunch.

Day2:Munnar Local Sightseeing
Day Highlights:
  • Munnar is set at an altitude of 6000 ft from the sea. Munnar is also called the "Kashmir of South India" and is a popular honeymoon destination.
  • After breakfast, proceed for local Sightseeing of Munnar - visit Rajamalai to see the Nilgiri Tahrs .Mattupetty where boating facility is available The Echo-point Kundale for boating in Shikara type of boats, Top Station - The border. Tata tea museum. Return to hotel in the evening for overnight stay.
Additional Info:

For proper execution, leave hotel at 8 am

Day3:Munnar to Alleppey - Kumarkom
Day Highlights:
  • After breakfast, check-out from the hotel. proceed to Alleppey, a small but famous town situated on the banks of Kerala backwaters. It is also famously know as "Venice of the East." or "Venetian Capital" of Kerala.
  • On arrival at Alleppey, check-in to your Houseboat follwed by Lunch. Spend your day relaxing on cruise. 
Additional Info:

Reach Houseboat before 12:30 pm (afternoon) for better cruising experience.

Day4:Alleppey - Kumarkom to Varkala (116 Kms / 3 Hrs)
Day Highlights:

After breakfast, depart from Kumarkom houseboat to Varkala. 

Varkala is the perfect place for a holiday getaway to enjoy golden beaches and is also rich with history. Places to visit: The Janardhanaswamy Temple, also known as Varkala Temple is a temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu built 20 centuries ago. The significant feature of the temple is the huge old bell which is considered an oblation to the temple. Timings are 4am to 12pm and from 5pm to 8pm. Entry is free. The Papanasam Beach, also known as Varkala Beach, is one of the best beaches in Kerala because of the view it offers from the cliff top. Like its name suggests, its water has medicinal properties and is believed to wash off one's impurities and sins. The Samadhi situated within the premises of the beach is the resting place of the great philosopher, Sree Narayana Guru and attracts many devotees. The Kappil Beach, a little away from Varkala, is a beach-backwater destination. The road splitting the two waterbodies [west is the majestic Arabian sea and to the east is the calm and serene backwaters]; the backwater and the Arabian Sea, is a treat to the eye.

Additional Info:

Leave houseboat by 10 am

Day5:Varkala to Kovalam (60 Kms / 1.5 Hrs)
Day Highlights:

After breakfast, spend sometime at beach and proceed to Kovalam. En-route visit Shanghumukham beach. Later proceed to Kovalam for hotel check-in. Relax at resort after check-in and proceed to visit Kovalam local beaches: Lighthouse Beach, Hawah Beach, Samudra Beach.

Spend your evening relaxing at your resort.

Additional Info:
Day6:Kovalam - Trivandrum Local
Day Highlights:

After breakfast, proceed to The Padmanabhaswamy temple. The principal deity Padmanabhaswamy (Vishnu) is enshrined in the "Anantha Shayana" posture, the eternal yogic sleep on the serpent Adi Shesha. Padmanabhaswamy is the tutelary deity of the royal family of Travancore. The titular Maharaja of Travancore, Moolam Thirunal Rama Varma, is the trustee of the temple. Later proceed to Poovar Island. You can enjoy boating in the beautiful backwaters of poovar. Visit various sightseeing places like mangroves forest, Mother Mary statue, elephant rock, coconut island, floating restaurant, floating cottages. While returning, visit Vzhinjam Marine aquarium. Return to your resort for overnight stay.

Additional Info:
Day Notes:

Carry Saree and Mundu (White lungi) while visiting Temples.

Day7:Departure
Day Highlights:

After breakfast, relax for a while and proceed to Trivandrum city. You can continue with shopping before airport drop.
