Day2:GANGTOK (TRIP TO TSOMGO LAKE + BABA MANDIR)(NATHULA PASS OPTIONAL RS4K/CAR)
Day Highlights:

This morning start for a half-day trip to tsomgo lake (12400ft), which is only

32kms from Gangtok city, the lake is almost about 1 km long and 15 mtrs deep.

The lake is considered sacred by the local people, further 12kms visit the baba

Harbhajan Singh mandir. Evening free at Gangtok for shopping and individual

Activity. Night halt at Gangtok hotel.

Day Highlights:

This morning after breakfast visit Tashi view point, Ganesh Tak, hanuman tak

Flower show, do-dul Chorten, bakthang falls, Gunjan monastery, Banjakri

Falls. Night halt at Gangtok.

Day Highlights:

Day5:Darjeeling Sightseeing
Day Highlights:

Early morning (at 04:00 am) drive to Tiger hill (8,400 ft.) To watch the

Spectacular sunrise over mt. Khangchendzongha (28,208 ft. Worlds 3rd highest

Peak), on your way back visit Batasia loop. After breakfast visit Himalayan

Mountaineering institute, p.n. zoological park (Thursday closed), Tenzing Rock,

Tibetan refugee self-help centre (Sunday closed), tea garden (outer view) and

Ropeway. Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

  • Stay
  • Sightseeing

  • Breakfast/dinner
Day6:DARJEELING TO ROCK GARDEN - EXCURSION
Day Highlights:

Distance: 98 km travel time: 4 hours 10 minutes

After having morning breakfast we will take you to rock garden is also know

As barbotey rock garden, where you can find a natural waterfall, scenic view

Point. Narrow trekking point. You can visit Ganga Maya park, enjoy boating

( extra charges applicable), find some beautiful species of flowers, enjoy

Waterfalls etc after that back to the hotel. Evening back to Darjeeling.

Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day7:DARJEELING TO NJP STN. /BAGDOGRA AIRPORT VIA MIRIK LAKE
Day Highlights:

After breakfast check out of your hotel and travel back to njp/ixb for your

Onward journey. On the way visit mirik and Pashupati market (Nepal). As you go

Home let the everlasting memory of the radiant Himalayas and this beautiful

Serene kingdom linger in your mind and prompt you now and again to revisit

This place.
