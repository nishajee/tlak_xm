Day3:Leh To Nubra valley via Khardung La
Day Highlights:

Early morning, leave on road to Nubra Valley via Khardongla Pass which is situated at a height of 18360 ft and considered as the highest motorable pass of the world and it is the perfect spot to capture the Mother Nature. In the afternoon, after having your lunch at some restaurant in Nubra Valley and Check in Guest House. Nubra has acquired fame for its sand dunes, the cold desert that surrounds it, Bactrian camels and its breathtaking natural beauty. Bactrian camels are another attraction. Native to the steppes of central Asia, the Bactrian camel has two humps, in contrast to the single-humped camels found in Rajasthan and other parts of India. The Bactrian camels were the main mode of transport when Ladakh was an important stopover on the Ancient trade routes with Central Asia. Stay overnight at Camps/Guest House. Afternoon enjoy the beauty of sand dune and double hump camel..

Day4:- Nubra - Pangong -Leh
Day Highlights:

After breakfast visit Deskit Monastery (Big Buddha), where you can have a magnificent view of Nubra Valley and later drive to pangong via shayok river with amazing landscape, adventure, stop lunch at Durbuk and 1 hr continue drive to pangong lake.Looking at pristine Pangong Lake, also known as Pangong Tso, you could be forgiven for thinking it's anywhere but India. At a literally breathtaking elevation of 14,270 feet (4,350 meters) above sea level, the lake presents a surreal spectacle. You will then visit Shooting Point (3 Idiots movie shoot) and View point (panoramic view of the lake) of the lake for some selfies.Post lunch drive back to Leh.enroute you will visit Thiksey monastery, Kharu, shey Sindhu ghat, Druk Padma, karpo(Rancho School).Dinner and overnight stay in Leh
