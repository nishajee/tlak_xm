KASHMIR 06 N / 07 D

Day1:Homeport – Srinagar
Day Highlights:

Arrive at Srinagar airport and transfer to hotel arrive and check in at Hotel Royal Batoo / Similar. In the evening enjoy the famous Shikara Ride for 01 Hour in Dal Lake. Overnight at Srinagar.

Day2:Srinagar – Sonmarg – Srinagar
Day Highlights:

After breakfast, proceed to Sonmarg for a day trip. Visit the Thajiwas Glacier (own cost) at an altitude of 9186 feet. It is a lovely vantage point to observe snow and the picturesque Kashmir Valley. Later return back to Srinagar in the evening.

Day3:Srinagar – Pahalgam
Day Highlights:

After breakfast, enjoy the beauty of Srinagar by visiting Shiv Temple, Golf Course, (on own). Later check out and proceed to Pahalgam. Enroute enjoy the charismatic beauty of Kashmir. Arrive and check in at Hotel Wild Flower  / Similar. Evening free for leisure activities.Overnight at Pahalgam.

Day4:Pahalgam - Gulmarg
Day Highlights:

After breakfast, check out and proceed to Gulmarg. Arrive and check in at Hotel Affarwat/ Similar. Later proceed for Gondola ride. You have the option to do the sledging, Horse riding, ATV biking, etc. (own cost). Overnight at Gulmarg.

Day5:Gulmarg - Srinagar
Day Highlights:

After breakfast, check out and proceed to Srinagar. Arrive and check in at Deluxe Houseboat.Later proceedfor local sightseeing which includes Shalimar Garden, Nishant Garden, Shankaracharya Hills and Moghul Garden which is popularly known as ChashmeShahi Garden. Overnight in Houseboat.

Day Highlights:

After Breakfast, check out and proceed to Dhood Pathri for the whole day tour. Evening free for leisure activities. Overnight Srinagar at Hotel Golden Tulip.
