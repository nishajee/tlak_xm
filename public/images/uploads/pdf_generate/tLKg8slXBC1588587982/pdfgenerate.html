HIMACHAL TOUR PACKAGE FOR FAMILY 5N/ 6 Days

Day1:Chandigarh to Manali
Day Highlights:

On arrival at Chandigarh Railway Station / Airport, arrival assistance by representative and arrival transfer from Chandigarh to Manali. Overnight at Hotel.

Additional Info:
Day2:Manali Sightseeing
Day Highlights:

After breakfast sightseeing of places in and around Manali, Like Hadimba temple,  Vashisht Bath, Van Vihar and evening shopping at the local market. O/N Hotel

Additional Info:
Day3:Manali Excursion to Kullu and Manikaran Sahib
Day Highlights:

Excursion to Kullu and Manikaran Sahib famous for its Sikh shrine and Hot Sulphur springs, on the way back shopping at Kullu. O/N Hotel  

Additional Info:
Day4:Manali -Bir-Dharamshala:
Day Highlights:

Morning drive to Dharamshala. On the way visit Bir Billing. Guests can do paragliding on their own.

Additional Info:
Day5:Dharamshala Sightseeing
Day Highlights:

After breakfast visit Local sightseeing , His holiness Dalai lama residence at Mcleodganj, War memorial, Bhagsunath temple and Dal Lake. O/N Hotel

Additional Info:
Day6:Dharmshala to Chandigarh drop
Day Highlights:

After breakfast checkout from hotel and drive to Chandigarh Railway station/Airport.
