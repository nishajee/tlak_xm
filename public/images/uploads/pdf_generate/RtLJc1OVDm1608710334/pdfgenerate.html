SCENIC KERALA

Day1:Arrival At Cochin Airport & Transfer To Munnar
Day Highlights:

Arrive at Cochin airport or station drive to Munnar (04 HRS). On the way visit vallara & cheeyapara falls & Kodanad ( If Open ) Later check-in to the hotel and rest whole day will be free for leisure. Overnight stay at hotel.

Additional Info:
Day2:Munnar Local Sightseeing
Day Highlights:

After breakfast go for local sightseeing of Munnar where you can visit Mattupatty Dam, Echo Point, Tea Plantation visit, Rajmalai, Ervikulam National Park. Evening back to hotel.Overnight stay at hotel.   

Additional Info:
Day3:Munnar To Thekkady
Day Highlights:

After breakfast check-out from hotel and processed for the Thekkady which is around 125kms (4hrs) drive from Munnar. Later check-in to the hotel and visit Periyar Wild life sanctuary ( Extra Cost ) and later go for spices plantation and shopping { Extra Cost }. Evening back to hotel.Overnight stay at hotel.                   

Additional Info:
Day4:Thekkady To Alleppey
Day Highlights:

After breakfast check-out from hotel and processed for the Alleppey on the way visit Jatayu Earth's Center . Later enjoy at back waters Cruises (Extra Cost). Late evening back to hotel.Overnight stay at hotel.      

Additional Info:

Optional Tour atayu Earth's Center

Day5:Departure
Day Highlights:

After Early morning breakfast check out from hotel and processed for Cochin which is 100kms (2hrs) drive from Alleppey for your flight or train for your home with lots of beautiful memories of tours.
