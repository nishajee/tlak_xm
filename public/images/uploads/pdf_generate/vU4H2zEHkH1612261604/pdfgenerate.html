Day3:Full Day South Goa Siteseeing
Day Highlights:

South Goa After breakfast, embark on a full-day South Goa sightseeing tour. The tour starts with visit to Shri Mangueshi Temple, one of the most visited temple in Goa. Following the temple, continue towards the churches and convents of Old Goa (UNESCO World Heritage Site) like Basilica of Bom Jesus, Church of Our Lady and Se Cathedral.

 

Post lunch, visit the idyllic Dona Paula Bay to enjoy spectacular views of the Mormugao Harbour. Later take a walk at the Miramar Beach.

 

Make your holiday in Goa more memorable by indulging in street shopping at the Panjim Market. In the evening, you can also go for river boat cruise ride at the Mandovi River (at extra cost and permitted you have time to cover it)

Additional Info:

Vechiel would be available between 9Am to 6 PM only

Day4:North Goa and Drop at Airport
Day Highlights:

North GOA: Embark on a full-day North Goa sightseeing tour. During this tour, you will get to visit some of the best beaches of Goa like Baga, Candolim, Calangute and Anjuna.

The first attraction as part of the tour is the ancient Fort Aguada, constructed in 1612 to protect Goa from enemy attacks. The fort and its lighthouse, would be an ideal place to click beautiful pictures.

 

After Fort Aguada, you will be visiting following beaches in order: Sinquerim Beach, Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, Anjuna Beach (famous for its white sand and red laterite rocks) and Vagator Beach. At Sinquerim or Calangute Beach, you can enjoy a myriad of water sports (own expense) such as water skiing, parasailing, etc.

You can enjoy a picturesque sunset from Chapora Fort, very near to Vagator Beach

Drop to Goa Airport
