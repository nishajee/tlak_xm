Darjeeling tour of Bijoy Biswas

Day1:Transfer to Darjeeling
Day Highlights:

Pickup at NJP Railway Station and transfer to Darjeeling. On arrival check in to your hotel. Rest of the day at leisure for your own activities.

Additional Info:

You will cross the beautiful Mahananda wildlife on your way to Darjeeling. The journey will take you three hours via Rohini, Kurseong, Sonada and Ghum. Enjoy the beautiful scenary all around.

Day Notes:

We recommend a vist to Mall Road in the evening. Enjoy a cup of tea a Chowrasta and savour the beautiful view of Kanchenjunga. Try the famous cakes of Glenary's and shop for winter garments at the nearby shops.

Day2:Darjeeling sightseeing
Day Highlights:

After breakfast start for the seven point sightseeing tour of Darjeeling.

Additional Info:

Your driver will report at 9 am. He will take you to all the popular sightseeing points of Darjeeling popularly knowns as the 7 points. 

Day Notes:

Darjeeling Zoo, Mountaineering institure, Tenzing rock, Tea Garden, Ropeway, Peace Pagoda and Tibetan Refugee centre comprise of the seven point sightseeing tour. Some points might be unavailaable due to the prevailingCovid situation. Your driver will guide you on it. Please note that there is no refund or compensation for missed points for any reason. 

Day3:Transfer to NJP Railway Station
Day Highlights:

After breakfast check out from the hotel and proceed for NJP Ralway Station. Tour ends.

Additional Info:
Day Notes:

Enroute visit Mirik. You can enjoy boating at the lake.
