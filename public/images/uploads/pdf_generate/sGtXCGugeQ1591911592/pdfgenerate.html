2 Days -In City Of Lakes- Udaipur

Day1:Arrival at Udaipur
Day Highlights:

Revel the royal vibes of Rajasthan and see the abodes of yesteryear kings on this holiday. Visit Saheliyon-Ki-Bari heritage garden in Udaipur and savour the Rajasthani folk art at the Museum of Folk Arts. Take a tour of the City Palace and see a mesmerizing sunset from the Monsoon Palace. Admire the Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur, along with Jaswant Thada. Let the Thar Desert leave you breathless when you are in Jaisalmer. Do not miss the sunset by Gadisagar Lake.

Additional Info:
Day2:Explore Udaipur
Day Highlights:

Udaipur, formerly the capital of the Mewar Kingdom, is a city in the western Indian state of Rajasthan.
